About Me

I'm a second-year MBA candidate at Stanford GSB and a former US Navy Cryptologic Warfare Officer. I began my career at the National Security Agency, specializing in low-level network exploitation for both offensive and defensive cyber operations. I then supported maritime cryptology in the Pacific. Before active duty I earned a BS in Computer Science from the US Naval Academy, where I discovered my love of running ultramarathons. I'm married, have a dog, and am a Christian - now you know almost everything about me.
