After college, I wanted to resume reading for pleasure and track the books I read. I created a Goodreads account, which was an okay experience, but I also found myself organizing my reading lists manually. Below is most of what I’ve read since college, sorted by the author’s last name. A handful are childhood re-reads - though not Harry Potter, which I first read in 2022. I’m still not over it.
